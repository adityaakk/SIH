Biology Topics

Cell Biology

Study of cell structure and function, exploring the basic building blocks of all living organisms.

Genetics

Examines genes, genetic variation, and heredity in living organisms, including DNA and inheritance patterns.

Ecology

Investigates the relationships between living organisms and their physical environment.

Evolution

Studies how species change over time through natural selection and genetic drift.

Anatomy & Physiology

Explores the structure and function of the body systems in humans and other animals.

Microbiology

Focuses on microorganisms including bacteria, viruses, archaea, fungi, and protozoa.

Biology Projects

DNA Extraction

Plant Growth Experiments

Test how different factors like light, water, or soil type affect plant growth and development.

Microscope Exploration

Collect samples from various environments and examine them under a microscope to discover microorganisms.

Amazing Biology Facts

If uncoiled, the DNA in all the cells in your body would stretch about 10 billion miles, beyond Pluto.

The human brain has about 86 billion neurons and can generate about 23 watts of power when awake.

Your heart beats about 100,000 times a day, pumping approximately 2,000 gallons of blood.

There are more microorganisms in one teaspoon of soil than there are people on Earth.

Humans share about 60% of their DNA with bananas and 98% with chimpanzees.

The surface area of human lungs is approximately equal to that of a tennis court.
