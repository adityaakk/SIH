Amazing Physics Facts

1

Quantum Entanglement

Two quantum particles can become entangled, meaning their properties are linked regardless of distance. Changing one instantly affects the other, even if they're light-years apart.

2

Time Dilation

According to Einstein's theory of relativity, time passes slower for objects moving at high speeds. Astronauts on the ISS age slightly slower than people on Earth!

3

The Double-Slit Experiment

Light behaves as both a particle and a wave. When not observed, light creates an interference pattern like a wave, but when measured, it behaves like particles.

4

Black Holes

Black holes have such intense gravity that not even light can escape them. They can warp space-time so severely that time nearly stops at the event horizon.
